We use only family-friendly fabrics. Additionally, these fabrics have a function of repelling any spills & stains which may occur during the usage.

Any furniture products available for purchase can be delivered upon your request. Custom orders will arrive at your location in nearly 6-8 weeks.

Yes. We also provide custom furniture manufacturing service. Contact us to discuss what item you need, your ideas, design, fabric, measurements.

We manufacture all furniture products from natural wood. We currently use such wood types like cherry, pine tree, oak, maple, and other hardwoods.

It depends on the type of furniture. Some products can be disassembled to facilitate transportation and movement; others cannot.
